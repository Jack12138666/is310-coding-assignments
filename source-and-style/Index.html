Ultraman Fighting Evolution

Ultraman Fighting Evolution: Ultraman Fighting Evolution for PlayStation is a classic fighting game that features a variety of Ultraman characters, each with unique abilities and special moves. The game includes single-player and multiplayer modes, with battles set in different arenas inspired by locations from the Ultraman series.

Ultraman Fighting Evolution 1 is a fighting game developed and published by Banpresto, released on February 19, 1998, for the PlayStation 1 platform.
